Frequently Asked Questions

How much does interior design typically cost?
The cost depends on your project size, material selection, and customization level. We offer flexible packages to fit your budget while ensuring premium quality and design.
Can I be involved during the design process?
Absolutely! We encourage client collaboration throughout every step, from concept sketches to final execution.
Can you work within my budget?
Yes, our team carefully plans the design to align with your budget and preferences — without compromising on aesthetics or quality.
Do you provide 3D design visualizations?
Yes! We create realistic 3D renderings so you can visualize your space before implementation begins.
How long does a typical interior project take?
The duration varies based on the scale of the project — generally between 3 to 8 weeks for residential interiors.
